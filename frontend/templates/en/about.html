About Wise Oracle

Wise Oracle is a cultural website offering three tools for self-reflection, drawn from traditional Chinese wisdom: oracle signs, the daily Chinese almanac, and BaZi (Four Pillars) birth chart readings.

Our Position

We present these traditions as cultural frameworks for self-examination — a mirror, not a map. Wise Oracle is not fortune telling, destiny reading, or life prediction. The aim is to offer a quiet moment to sit with a question and reflect on your own patterns.

The Three Tools

  • Ask the Oracle: draw one of 384 Zhuge Oracle signs using three words or three numbers you offer. Each sign carries a traditional verse and interpretations across career, wealth, relationships, health, study, and general outlook.
  • Daily Chinese Almanac: today's favorable and unfavorable activities, lunar date, solar term, and zodiac — a traditional guide to the rhythms of the day.
  • Birth Chart Reading: explore your BaZi birth chart as a cultural framework for self-reflection, with AI-generated reflection prompts positioned as one perspective among many.

Responsible Use

All readings are for entertainment, cultural exploration, and self-reflection only. They are not medical, legal, financial, psychological, or life-critical advice. Different traditions offer different interpretations; consider this one perspective among many.
