About Wise Oracle

Our Mission

Wise Oracle brings traditional Chinese cultural wisdom — oracle signs, daily almanac, and BaZi birth chart — to English-speaking audiences as a tool for self-reflection, not fortune telling.

What we believe

These traditions are mirrors for self-examination, not predictions of fate. We present them with cultural authenticity while being clear about their entertainment and reflective purpose.

Algorithm and AI

Oracle signs are drawn using the traditional Zhuge Oracle method: three inputs are transformed into a number that maps to one of 384 signs. Birth chart readings use AI as a cultural interpretation layer, not as a predictor.

Cultural positioning

This is not New Age, cosmic energy, or manifestation. It is traditional Chinese cultural practice presented honestly for those curious about its reflective possibilities.
