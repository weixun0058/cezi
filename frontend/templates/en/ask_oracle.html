How were these numbers formed?

For Three Words: each word is converted to a number using A=1, B=2, …, Z=26. The letter sums are reduced to single digits (a sum ending in 0 becomes 1), then composed into a three-digit number that maps to one of 384 oracle signs.

For Three Numbers: the three numbers (each 0–999) are combined into a single seed, which is then mapped to one of 384 signs. All three numbers cannot be zero.
