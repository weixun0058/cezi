### 签文诗（sign_text）

**中文原文**：
事未宽，心不安，疑虑久，始安然。

**英文翻译**：
Matters not yet eased, heart ill at rest;
Doubts long harbored, at last they find peace.

---

### 整体解读（interpretation1）

**中文原文**：
签文“事未宽，心不安，疑虑久，始安然”，全篇以心路历程为轴，道出从困顿至平复之机。首句“事未宽”喻事态尚未舒展，如行狭径，不可急切；“心不安”点明由此而生的烦乱，乃人情之常。第三句“疑虑久”是全签关键——疑非坏事，久则沉淀，恰是拨云见日的必经历程；末句“始安然”终得宁定，如夜尽天明。此签卦象暗合坎卦，坎为险陷，亦主智慧，正应“疑中悟道，久后心安”之象。整体寓意：面对不解之局，宜以时间换空间，消解猜疑，静待自明，不可强求立解。

**英文翻译**：
The journey from turmoil to calm is traced through these lines. Initial unease gives way to clarity as doubt, long held, becomes a vessel for understanding. This hexagram suggests that patience and reflection turn confusion into peace.

### 事业（career）

**中文原文**：
事业对应“事未宽”，主当下事态胶着，或遇信息阻滞、方案难决。签中“疑虑久”更显职场人际可能有隐情未明，或对抉择反复掂量。此象并非凶兆，反是提醒：宜暂缓步伐，全面核实，如暗中摸石过河，待细节明朗，自得“始安然”。若逢考核、竞聘，前期多波折，后期反能脱颖而出。宜：深耕专业，等待明确指令或贵人指路；忌：躁进争功，轻信流言，或强行推动未成熟计划。

**英文翻译**：
Career mirrors 'matters not yet eased' — current situations may be stagnant, with information unclear or decisions pending. 'Doubts long harbored' hints at hidden dynamics in workplace relationships or second-guessing choices. This is not an ill omen but an invitation to slow down, verify thoroughly, and wait for details to emerge. For evaluations or competitions, initial setbacks may yield later breakthroughs. Advisable to deepen expertise and await clear instructions or a supportive person; advisable to avoid rushing for credit, trusting rumors, or forcing immature plans.

### 财运（wealth）

**中文原文**：
财运依“事未宽”之象，主财路未畅，偏财难现。“心不安”反映对资金安全的忧虑，“疑虑久”更示投资决策易陷入反复。此签财星不显，宜守不宜攻，薪俸正财可保，外财勿念。后期“始安然”指守成之下，财务渐稳，小有积蓄。宜：保持现金流稳定，暂缓扩张；忌：跟风投机，涉足不明朗的合作，或为图利而担险。

**英文翻译**：
Wealth reflects 'matters not yet eased' — financial channels may be slow, windfall unlikely. 'Heart ill at rest' signals concern over liquidity, and 'doubts long harbored' suggests repeated hesitation in investment decisions. This sign indicates a defensive stance: stable salary income is dependable, but speculative gains are not foreseen. Later 'at last they find peace' implies steady savings through conservative management. Advisable to maintain cash flow and postpone expansion; advisable to avoid following trends, entering unclear partnerships, or taking risks for profit.

### 情感（love）

**中文原文**：
情感上，“事未宽”喻关系或有隔阂未消，“心不安”正是内心映射，易生焦虑。“疑虑久”直指双方可能因沟通欠缺而互相猜疑，或对前景信心不足。签意劝人勿轻言放弃，当以诚相待，历过疑云，方能“始安然”。单身者：暂缓表白，先释清暧昧；有伴者：忌翻旧账，宜选适当时机坦露心声。

**英文翻译**：
In love, 'matters not yet eased' points to unresolved tensions, and 'heart ill at rest' mirrors inner anxiety. 'Doubts long harbored' directly addresses mutual suspicion from poor communication or wavering confidence. The sign advises against giving up lightly; honesty can dissolve doubts, leading to eventual peace. Singles: delay confessions until ambiguity clears. Couples: avoid revisiting past grievances; choose a timely moment for sincere conversation.

### 健康（health）

**中文原文**：
此签身心互照，“事未宽”可比经脉不畅、气息不调；“心不安”乃心神浮越，卧难安枕；“疑虑久”易致思虑过度，暗耗气血。然“始安然”示静养可复。卦应坎水，需注意肾气与骨节，勿过劳。宜：早眠养神，晨起缓步舒筋，少食生冷护脾胃；需警惕：失眠多梦、头痛目眩、食欲波动。

**英文翻译**：
This sign reflects a mind-body connection: 'matters not yet eased' resembles blocked energy or disharmony; 'heart ill at rest' indicates restless sleep and mental agitation; 'doubts long harbored' may lead to overthinking and depletion. Yet 'at last they find peace' shows recovery through stillness. Corresponding to Kan (Water), it suggests attention to kidney energy and bones, avoiding overwork. Advisable: early sleep to restore spirit, gentle morning walks, and light diet to protect digestion. Watch for: insomnia, headaches, dizzy spells, appetite fluctuations.

**中文原文**：
学业恰如“事未宽”，主遇瓶颈，疑窦重重，如数学公式难解、文章要义不明。“疑虑久”正是独立思考的淬炼，虽耗时却固本。此签利深研，不利速成。考试运前期易错漏，后期复盘能得高分。宜：制定分阶计划，先攻根基再破难题；忌：随众换专业方向，或临考方悔准备不足。

Exam luck: early mistakes possible, but later review can yield high scores. Advisable: create a phased plan, master fundamentals before tackling tough problems; advisable to avoid switching fields casually or regretting insufficient preparation before exams.

### 总论（general）

**中文原文**：
此签乃先忧后安之兆，全赖“疑虑久”三字转化——疑非病，是通向清明的阶石。行动之要：一、遇事放缓，求稳不求快；二、惑时多方参证，忌独断；三、持守正念，信否极泰来。正如夜尽必曙，心若安时，事自宽。

As night must end, when the heart is calm, matters will ease. Reflect on how this resonates with your current situation.
