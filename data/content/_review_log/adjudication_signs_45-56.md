## 详细评定

### 第45签
- **[Medium] "Lu above Li" 卦象结构表述不准**：Gemini 指出旅卦是"上火下山"，写成"Lu above Li"易混淆卦名与单卦名。**接受**。但否决 Gemini 建议 "in every port"（那是第142签的事，与45签无关）。改为 "Lu (Traveling, represented by Fire over Mountain)"。

### 第46签
- **[Medium] sign_text 三行**：原文"奔波一世，总是虚浮，无常一到万事休，急早回头"是四句。**接受**，改为四行。

### 第47签
- **[Medium] "will eventually be felt"**：Gemini 指出这是对未来状态的预测。**接受**，但采用较温和的 "is bound to be felt over time"（而非 Gemini 的 "is poised to be felt"），因为"精诚所至，金石为开"有较强的必然性。
- **study 字段**：Gemini 称有 "you will be steady on the day"，但实际内容并无此句。Gemini 引用错误，**不修改**。

### 第48签
- **[High] "Zhen hexagram" 应为 "Zhen Palace"**：Gemini 正确指出大过卦属震宫，但不含震卦。**接受**，改为 "The energy of the Zhen Palace is active, yet the Da Guo hexagram warns against excess"。
- **[Medium] sign_text 三行**：**接受**，改为四行。

### 第49签
- **[High] 漏译"久病逢医""不佳者得良才"**：Gemini 指出这是核心断语。**接受**，补译为 "Historically, this sign indicates that those suffering from long-term illnesses are poised to meet an exceptional healer, while struggling businesses suggest a potential to recruit key talent for a prosperous turnaround."
- **[Medium] sign_text 三行**：**接受**，改为四行。
- **[Medium] "will arrive like thunder"**：**接受**，改为 "are poised to arrive like thunder"。

### 第50签
- **[Critical] "Bu Ya" 学术幻觉**：Gemini 正确指出易经无 "Bu Ya" 卦，应为"无妄（Wu Wang）"。**接受**，改为 "Pi transforming to Wu Wang (Innocence)"。同时修复了原文中"restrain your锋芒"的中文字符残留，改为 "restrain your edge"。
- **[Medium] "Exams will be average"**：**接受**，改为 "Exam outcomes are likely to be average"。

### 第51签
- **[High] 漏译"大器晚成""财运不祥""诉讼胜诉"**：Gemini 指出这三条是核心断语。**接受**，补译为 "The traditional guidance advises: success is likely to be a late bloom. If asking of wealth, the sign suggests caution as immediate prospects are unfavorable; if asking of litigation, it suggests that prolonged disputes are poised to eventually resolve in your favor."（用 "resolve in your favor" 软化"胜诉"）。

### 第52签
- **[Medium] "will melt"**：**接受**，改为 "are poised to melt"。
